!| scenario       |Add system                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
|$res=            |CLI|${CLIBatch} AddSystem --SystemName ${SystemName} --NetworkAddress ${SystemAddress} --PlatformName \"${SystemPlatform}\" --FunctionalAccount ${FunctAcct} --FuncAcctCred \"${FunctAcctPwd}\" --Timeout ${SystemTimeout} --PlatSpecificValue sudo|
|Verify_Contain_OR|$res;;successful                                                                                                                                                                                                          |

!|scenario    |Create Account on System                                                                                           |
|accountAction|${SubPlatform};;create;;${SystemAddress};;${FunctAcct};;${FunctAcctPwd};;${ManagedAccount};;${ManagedAccountPwd}|

!|scenario    |Change Account Password on System                                |NewPwd                                |
|accountAction|${SubPlatform};;password;;${SystemAddress};;${FunctAcct};;${FunctAcctPwd};;${ManagedAccount};;@NewPwd|

!|scenario    |Login Account to System                    |ManPwd                    |
|accountAction|${SubPlatform};;login;;${SystemAddress};;${ManagedAccount};;@ManPwd|

!|scenario    |Delete Account on System                                                                     |
|accountAction|${SubPlatform};;delete;;${SystemAddress};;${FunctAcct};;${FunctAcctPwd};;${ManagedAccount}|
