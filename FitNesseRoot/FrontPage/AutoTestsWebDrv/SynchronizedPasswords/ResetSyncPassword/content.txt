!define NewSyncPassword {gr@in41R}

!4 Create Sync Password
!|script                                                                                                                                            |
|Add Sync Password|${SyncPasswordName}|with value|${SincPwdInitialValue}|Check Profile|${SyncPwdCheckProfile}|Change Profile|${SyncPwdChangeProfile}|

!|script                                                                            |
