!* Test description
According to documentation: "Can only be selected if Approvals Required is greater than 1.
If selected, you can require that approvals for requests come from one or more groups."
So I planned to realize two subtests.
Subtest One: Only one group is selected - all approvals must come from members of this group. 
Subtest Two: More than one group is selected - at least 1 approval must come from each group.
But indeed TPAM requires at least 2 approvers and 2 groups selected. So only second test is realized.
*!
