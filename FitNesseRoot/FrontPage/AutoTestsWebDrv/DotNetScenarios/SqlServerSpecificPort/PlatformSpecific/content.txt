!|scenario    |Create Account on System                                                                                                    |
|accountAction|MSSQL_Server_Port;;create;;${SystemAddress};;${Port};;${FunctAcct};;${FunctAcctPwd};;${ManagedAccount};;${ManagedAccountPwd}|

!|scenario    |Change Account Password on System                                     |NewPwd                                    |
|accountAction|MSSQL_Server_Port;;password;;${SystemAddress};;${Port};;${FunctAcct};;${FunctAcctPwd};;${ManagedAccount};;@NewPwd|

!|scenario    |Delete Account on System                                                                              |
|accountAction|MSSQL_Server_Port;;delete;;${SystemAddress};;${Port};;${FunctAcct};;${FunctAcctPwd};;${ManagedAccount}|

!|scenario    |Login Account to System                         |ManPwd                        |
|accountAction|MSSQL_Server_Port;;login;;${SystemAddress};;${Port};;${ManagedAccount};;@ManPwd|

!| scenario       |Add system                                                                                                                                                                    |
|$res=            |NetAPI|${APIBatch} ${CommandTimeout} AddSystem ${SystemName} ${SystemAddress} "${SystemPlatform}" "${SystemDescription}" ${SystemTimeout} ${FunctAcct} ${FunctAcctPwd} ${Port}|
|Verify_Contain_OR|$res;;successful                                                                                                                                                              |
