!|scenario        |Create Account on System                                                                                                           |
|$res=            |PowerShellScript|manageLOCALusers.ps1 create ${SystemAddress} ${FunctAcct} ${FunctAcctPwd} ${ManagedAccount} "${ManagedAccountPwd}"|
|Verify_Contain_OR|$res;;successful                                                                                                                   |

!|scenario        |Change Account Password on System|NewPwd                                               |                                               |
|$res=            |PowerShellScript                 |manageLOCALusers.ps1 password ${SystemAddress} ${FunctAcct} ${FunctAcctPwd} ${ManagedAccount} @NewPwd|
|Verify_Contain_OR|$res;;successful                                                                                                                       |

!|scenario        |Login Account to System|ManPwd                               |                               |
|$res=            |PowerShellScript       |manageLOCALusers.ps1 login ${SystemAddress} ${ManagedAccount} @ManPwd|
|Verify_Contain_OR|$res;;successful                                                                             |

!|scenario        |Delete Account on System                                                                                    |
|$res=            |PowerShellScript|manageLOCALusers.ps1 delete ${SystemAddress} ${FunctAcct} ${FunctAcctPwd} ${ManagedAccount}|
|Verify_Contain_OR|$res;;successful                                                                                            |
